About This Visualization

The Problem: Traditional weather stations provide only ~15% global coverage, missing critical data over oceans and remote regions where 85% of weather phenomena develop.

WindBorne's Solution: A constellation of smart weather balloons that continuously collect atmospheric data across the entire globe, especially over oceans where traditional stations cannot reach.

What You're Seeing:

  • Green markers: WindBorne-only coverage - areas where no weather stations exist within 200km
  • Blue markers: Current WindBorne balloon positions
  • Red dots: Traditional weather stations (NOAA ISD network)
  • Gray lines: 24-hour flight paths showing balloon trajectories

Notice how WindBorne balloons concentrate over oceans and remote areas - precisely where traditional weather observation infrastructure cannot reach. This is the 85% gap they're filling.
